üçõ Chicken Tikka Masala

45 minutes
Serves 4
Intermediate
Watch Video Tutorial

Step-by-Step Instructions

Marinate the chicken: In a large bowl, combine yogurt, lemon juice, ginger-garlic paste, garam masala, turmeric, and salt. Add chicken pieces and mix well. Cover and refrigerate for at least 2 hours (or overnight for best results).

Prepare the sauce base: Heat oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add chopped onions and cook until golden brown, about 8-10 minutes. Add minced garlic and ginger, cook for another 2 minutes.

Add tomatoes and spices: Stir in pureed tomatoes, paprika, cumin, coriander powder, and chili powder. Cook for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the sauce thickens and oil starts to separate.

Cook the chicken: Remove chicken from marinade and add to a hot grill or grill pan. Cook for 3-4 minutes per side until charred and nearly cooked through. Cut into bite-sized pieces.

Combine and simmer: Add the grilled chicken pieces to the sauce. Pour in heavy cream and stir well. Simmer for 10-15 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.

Final touches: Add butter and kasuri methi (dried fenugreek leaves). Stir gently and cook for another 2-3 minutes. Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve hot with naan bread or basmati rice.
